Dedicated to impart up-to-date knowledge, The Mahatma Gandhi Shikshan Mandal Trust started in the year 1969. Established in the Chopda Taluk situated at the foot hill of satpuda ranges, which is one of the progressive taluka in the north Maharashtra region. Today the trust runs several colleges under its umbrella from preschool to post graduate level.
The mission of the sanstha is to provide professional, need based & quality education to the students of  rural & tribal area.
        With a successful spread of the institions, under the umbrella of  MGS Mandal, offers quality education from pre- school through teachers education to post graduate programme. Equipped with modern teaching aids housed in finest infrastructure and serene campus, MGS Mandals high calibre of faculties pave the way towards reaching great heights for bright and ambitious students of all its institutions.
