RANM(Revised Auxiliary Nurse Midwife)

Revised Auxiliary Nurse Midwife (R.A.N.M) with Female Health Worker (F.H.W) at AMFNI is an 18 month programme. The programme is divided into Theory and Clinical blocks:

Theory Blocks - During this period the student is expected to attend classes in the college Campus as per the rotation plan. The Theory portion will be covered in the form of Lectures, Lecture cum Demonstration, Seminars, assignments, projects etc.

Clinical Blocks - During this period the students will be posted in Hospital wards, Community set up or any other area approved for the experience. The student will be expected to provide care to the individuals or the families and complete assignments as outlined in the curriculum. More focus is given to community oriented training.50% is the Standard of passing in each subject in both Theory and Practical's.
